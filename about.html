About our Foundation

Our organization is a non-profitable organization. The main objectives of our organization are to help the children, poor people and provide a good life to them. We have been saved many children by providing them food, shelter, and education globally. Along with this, we put hand towards the people by providing employments. Otherwise, various social-welfare also conducted. Some of them are blood donation camps and plantation.

Many of the NGOs and non-NGOs also came on the collaboration with us. Our foundation believe in the support to the needy people. We want to hand-shake with the people who is willing to support and help us. And, we will be grateful and thankful for your donation.



Head of PNC Foundation,
